# Calendar PRD

Version: 1.0

---

# 1. Purpose

The Calendar is the operational hub for beauty professionals.

It enables professionals to manage their availability, appointments, working hours, and time efficiently while ensuring clients always see accurate availability.

---

# 2. Problem Statement

Many independent beauty professionals currently manage appointments through:

- Paper planners
- Instagram DMs
- WhatsApp
- Phone calls
- Multiple calendars

This often results in:

- Double bookings
- Missed appointments
- Manual scheduling
- Lost revenue
- Client frustration

Atlas centralizes scheduling into a single, reliable calendar.

---

# 3. Goals

The Calendar should:

- Display real-time availability.
- Prevent scheduling conflicts.
- Simplify schedule management.
- Support business growth.
- Reduce administrative work.

---

# 4. Non-Goals

The Calendar is not intended to:

- Replace personal productivity apps.
- Serve as a full business management suite.
- Handle accounting or payroll.

Its responsibility is schedule management.

---

# 5. User Stories

## Professional

As a professional I want to:

- Set my working hours.
- Block unavailable time.
- Manage appointments.
- View daily, weekly, and monthly schedules.
- Add breaks.
- Schedule vacations.
- Receive reminders.
- Avoid double bookings.

## Client

As a client I want to:

- See available appointment times.
- Book only available slots.
- Receive accurate availability information.

---

# 6. Functional Requirements

## Views

- Daily view
- Weekly view
- Monthly view
- Agenda view

---

## Availability

Professionals can configure:

- Working days
- Working hours
- Breaks
- Holidays
- Vacation periods
- Special schedules

---

## Appointment Management

Each appointment displays:

- Client name
- Service
- Duration
- Status
- Notes
- Booking source

---

## Blocking Time

Professionals can:

- Block custom time periods.
- Block entire days.
- Block recurring periods.

Blocked time is unavailable for booking.

---

## Notifications

The Calendar displays:

- Upcoming appointments
- Booking requests
- Schedule conflicts
- Reschedule requests
- Daily summaries

---

## Integrations (Future)

Potential integrations:

- Google Calendar
- Apple Calendar
- Outlook Calendar

---

# 7. User Flow

Professional opens Calendar

↓

Views today's schedule

↓

Accepts or manages bookings

↓

Blocks unavailable time if needed

↓

Calendar updates availability

↓

Clients see updated availability instantly
