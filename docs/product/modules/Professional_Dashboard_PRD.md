# Professional Dashboard PRD

Version: 1.0

---

# 1. Purpose

The Professional Dashboard is the operational command center for beauty professionals.

It provides a single place to manage appointments, clients, earnings, business performance, and daily operations.

The dashboard should answer one simple question:

"How is my business performing today?"

---

# 2. Problem Statement

Independent beauty professionals often manage their business across multiple tools:

- Instagram
- Booking apps
- Notes
- Excel
- Banking apps
- Messaging apps

This fragmentation wastes time and reduces visibility into business performance.

Atlas centralizes the business into one dashboard.

---

# 3. Goals

The Dashboard should:

- Give professionals a daily business overview.
- Surface important actions.
- Reduce administrative work.
- Help professionals grow revenue.
- Improve client retention.

---

# 4. Non-Goals

The Dashboard is not intended to:

- Replace accounting software.
- Become a CRM for large enterprises.
- Replace financial reporting tools.

Its responsibility is operational visibility.

---

# 5. User Stories

As a professional I want to:

- See today's appointments.
- View upcoming bookings.
- Monitor earnings.
- Track cancellations.
- View recent reviews.
- Respond to messages.
- Identify returning clients.
- See business performance at a glance.

---

# 6. Functional Requirements

## Today's Schedule

Display:

- Today's appointments
- Upcoming appointments
- Appointment status
- Client names
- Service names

---

## Business Overview

Display:

- Today's revenue
- Weekly revenue
- Monthly revenue
- Upcoming payouts
- Pending payments

---

## Clients

Display:

- New clients
- Returning clients
- Recent bookings
- Repeat booking rate

---

## Reviews

Display:

- Average rating
- Recent reviews
- Reviews requiring response

---

## Messages

Display:

- Unread conversations
- Booking questions
- Pending replies

---

## Performance

Display:

- Profile views
- Booking conversion
- Cancellation rate
- No-show rate

---

## Quick Actions

Professionals can quickly:

- Add blocked time
- Update availability
- Open calendar
- Open messages
- View bookings
- Edit profile
