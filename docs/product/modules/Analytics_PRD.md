# Analytics PRD

Version: 1.0

---

# 1. Purpose

Analytics provides professionals with actionable insights about their business performance.

Unlike the Dashboard, which focuses on daily operations, Analytics helps professionals understand trends, identify opportunities, and make better business decisions over time.

---

# 2. Problem Statement

Most independent beauty professionals know how busy they feel but struggle to answer questions like:

- Which services generate the most revenue?
- Which months are the busiest?
- How many new clients become returning clients?
- Which marketing efforts actually bring bookings?
- Why are cancellations increasing?

Without reliable data, business decisions are based on intuition rather than evidence.

---

# 3. Goals

Analytics should help professionals:

- Understand business performance.
- Increase revenue.
- Improve client retention.
- Optimize pricing.
- Improve scheduling.
- Identify growth opportunities.

---

# 4. Non-Goals

Analytics is not intended to:

- Replace professional accounting software.
- Become a BI platform.
- Provide tax reporting.

Its purpose is business intelligence for daily decision-making.

---

# 5. User Stories

As a professional I want to:

- Track revenue trends.
- Understand booking patterns.
- Measure client retention.
- See cancellation rates.
- Understand profile performance.
- Compare different time periods.
- Identify my best-performing services.

---

# 6. Functional Requirements

## Revenue Analytics

Display:

- Daily revenue
- Weekly revenue
- Monthly revenue
- Yearly revenue
- Revenue trends

---

## Booking Analytics

Display:

- Total bookings
- Completed bookings
- Cancelled bookings
- No-show rate
- Booking growth

---

## Client Analytics

Display:

- New clients
- Returning clients
- Repeat booking rate
- Client lifetime value (future)

---

## Service Analytics

Display:

- Most booked services
- Highest revenue services
- Average appointment duration
- Average service price

---

## Profile Analytics

Display:

- Profile views
- Profile-to-booking conversion
- Saves
- Follows
- Messages received

---

## Review Analytics

Display:

- Average rating
- Review trend
- Reviews per month
- Response rate

---

## Filters

Users can filter analytics by:

- Date range
- Service
- Booking status

---
